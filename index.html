About Me

My name is Kasim Francisco Abe, and I am 18 years old. I graduated from Ecole Dr. Charles Best Secondary School in the summer of 2022 from the French immersion program.
I come from a Ugandan and Filipino background, my dad being from Uganda and my mom from The Philippines. some of my interests reside in music, technology, and especially sports. Basketball is my favourite sport to play. Although it may not be my first, it was the sport truly fell in love with.
